ABOUT ME

My name is Emma, and I'm a second-year AGPM major! However, I'm honestly a bit of a beginner when it comes to gaming, I grew up playing some games on my Nintendo DS and XBox 360, and I've pretty much stuck with those same games until now with a few exceptions. Some of the most well known games I grew up playing are Minecraft and New Super Mario Bros, and a lesser known one is ilomilo. Currently I've been playing Breath of the Wild and Skyrim. Some other random facts about me are that I love to thrift and make jewelry. Fashion is one of my favorite creative outlets, I like to upcycle things by using scissors, ribbon/yarn/fabric, and sewing/safety pins to make boring stuff look cool. I'm from the East Bay, specifically Concord, and one thing I miss about it is all the good stores/restaurants there were. I also miss having a car and being able to easily get to places and take trips with friends. My favorite food is banh mi, a Vietnamese sandwich. I also really like lychee or peach flavored things.
